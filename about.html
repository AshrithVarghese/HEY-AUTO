About Us

Our Vision

Founded in 2023, Hey! Auto is committed to revolutionizing urban transportation by providing a reliable and convenient autorickshaw booking service. Our mission is to make daily commuting hassle-free and affordable. With a team of dedicated professionals and state-of-the-art technology, we strive to offer a seamless experience for our users, ensuring safety, comfort, and punctuality. At Hey!Auto, we are passionate about connecting people and places, one ride at a time.
